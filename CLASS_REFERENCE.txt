━━━ ALL DEBUFFS (Status Effects) ━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

 1. BURNING         [DoT]              6% of target's max HP as fire damage per turn.

 2. POISONED        [DoT — Stacking]   4% of target's max HP per stack per turn.
                                        Stacks up to 5× (max 20%/turn).

 3. BLEEDING        [DoT]              5% of target's max HP as bleed damage per turn.

 4. BLINDED         [Accuracy]         50% chance to miss attacks each turn.

 5. SHOCKED         [Stun]             50% chance to be stunned (skip turn) each turn.

 6. FREEZING        [Damage debuff]    Enemy physical attacks deal reduced damage.

 7. PETRIFIED       [Damage debuff]    Enemy magic attacks deal reduced damage.

 8. WEAKENED        [Defense debuff]   Target's DEF reduced by 20% (both pDEF & mDEF).

 9. DOOM            [Delayed execute]  After duration expires: instant kill if below 30% HP.

10. WANING          [Stat drain]       −2 to a random stat each turn for duration.

11. DEATH MARK      [Crit vulnerability]  All hits against the marked target are guaranteed crits.

12. HASTUR'S MARK   [Debuff amplifier] All debuffs deal 50% more damage for duration.

13. JUDGMENT MARK   [Vulnerability]    Target takes 20% more damage from all sources.

━━━ QUICK CLASS PICK GUIDE ━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  Want BIG DAMAGE?        → Scholar (INT nukes) or Brute (STR scaling)
  Want to NEVER DIE?      → Warden (shields, heals, barriers)
  Want CRITS & SPEED?     → Shadowblade (AGI, evasion, poison stacking)
  Want CHAOS & DEBUFFS?   → Mad Prophet (gambling, madness, every debuff)
  Want EASY/STRAIGHTFORWARD? → Brute (high HP, simple rotation)
  Want HIGH SKILL CEILING?   → Scholar or Mad Prophet (glass cannon play)
